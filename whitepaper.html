MetaRupee (MRP) - Whitepaper

Overview

MetaRupee (MRP) is a decentralized digital token developed on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20). It is designed for fast, low-fee, and secure transactions with a vision to empower financial growth in India and emerging markets.

Token Details

  • Name: MetaRupee
  • Symbol: MRP
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000 MRP
  • Blockchain: Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)
  • Decimals: 18
  • Owner: Aman Khan

Vision

Our goal is to provide a trusted and easy-to-use digital currency that supports borderless payments, community rewards, and future integrations in DeFi and NFTs.
