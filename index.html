About MetaRupee

MetaRupee (MRP) is a decentralized token built on Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20) designed for fast, secure, and low-cost transactions. Focused on making crypto accessible in India and beyond.

Token Information

  • Symbol: MRP
  • Blockchain: Binance Smart Chain (BEP-20)
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000 MRP (10 Million)
  • Decimals: 18
  • Owner: Aman Khan
